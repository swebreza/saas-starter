## Studio 24 · Executive Overview

### Product Thesis
Creators and growth marketers waste time stitching together prompts, editors, and analytics across disconnected tools. **Studio 24** replaces that chaos with an AI-native studio that turns a single idea into ready-to-post campaigns for every major platform within minutes.

### What We Deliver
- **Instant Creative Engine**: Gemini-tailored hooks, scripts, long-form outlines, and caption packs tuned to brand voice, audience, and platform nuances.
- **Social Video Accelerator**: Repurpose YouTube links or transcripts into shorts playbooks, teaser scripts, and multi-hook bundles built for TikTok, Reels, Shorts, and beyond.
- **Auto-Reels Renderer**: Turn any YouTube video into multiple downloadable, premium-formatted shorts with speaker-aware framing, animated overlays, and captions for Premium plans.
- **Storyboard-to-Visual Bridge**: Structured scene plans with one-click Canva handoffs ensure Premium customers go straight from strategy to export-ready visuals.
- **Usage-aware Monetization**: Stripe-managed Free vs Premium tiers keep gross margin above 80% while aligning entitlements with generation cost.

### Strategic Positioning
- **Tagline**: “Idea in — multi-platform content out in minutes.”
- **Brand Voice**: Confident, insight-led, premium. We sell in USD to clients expecting agency-grade polish.
- **Proof Focus**: Output quality, cross-channel breadth, and time-to-publish trump raw feature checklists.

### Experience Flywheel
1. **Capture**: Landing experience and dashboard banners steer users toward Text, Video, or Storyboard studios tied to their goals.
2. **Configure**: Every studio guides inputs—tone, platform, audience—so Gemini prompts stay on-brief.
3. **Generate**: Server-only Gemini calls return structured JSON for consistent UI rendering and easy export.
4. **Refine & Export**: Copy buttons, Canva template handoffs, and plan-aware upsells help teams finish assets without context switching.
5. **Upsell & Retain**: Stripe-powered upgrades, usage meters, and “unlock more channels” education loops drive conversion from Free to Premium—especially into automated reels exports.

### Pricing Lens
- **Free**: 10 AI generations/day, Text Studio access, storyboard previews locked behind Premium CTA.
- **Premium (USD)**: Unlimited runs across studios, Canva handoffs, saved projects, white-label exports, priority prompts.
- **Future Monetization**: Volume add-ons for agencies, team workspaces, brand libraries, and campaign analytics.
